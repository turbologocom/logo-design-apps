Best Logo Design Software Apps

These days, when a consumer society takes place, representing a company with a logo is a highly important factor for its success, regardless of it being small or big. The question arises, why? There are a few reasons for it. First things first, it establishes a fundamental impression of a business. Secondly, it is a point that makes a firm recognizable. Plus, it represents a whole concept of a product/ service it provides. In consequence a problem appears: how exactly to make it? And, of course, there is an efficacious solution.

Logo design software – is a way to create that kind of a particular symbol. Probably, you already heard about Canvas, but there exist a lot of alternatives that do not yield in functioning to it.

The list of the most useful logo design software programs of 2021 follows.

Turbologo

Turbologo is probably the greatest logo design app presented on the market today. It includes a huge icon and image library, different color options, vector logo files, and many more. With this tool one can achieve successful branding in no time. Not only does it happen thanks to its user-friendliness and high customization opportunities with a wide range of features, but also its beautiful collection of templates. Therefore, it is faithfully considered to be the easiest and the fastest logo maker.

Another Turbologo advantage is its affordability: depending on a chosen package, pricing varies from $3 to $9. This makes it quite rational to small enterprises, the amount of which is continuously growing.

Adobe Illustrator

Adobe has been present on the visual design market since 1985, which makes it extremely experienced and trustworthy. Adobe Illustrator is a part of its suit. This best logo maker takes care so that you could create icons, draw images, and logos as well. It provides rich features opportunities so that it is possible to build nearly any graphic art object.

At Adobe Illustrator you can zoom in or out your project at any stage of work. It supports both Windows and Mac PC systems. Comes with more than ten thousand fonts and allows editing several arias simultaneously. The pricing can vary depending on the time you are going to use the app. The monthly payment is $31.49, yet if purchasing an annual plan for $239.88, it turns out to be $20.99 per month.

Inkscape

Web-based Free

  • lots of adjustment instrument

Inkscape is an online open-source logo builder. It is a great decision when it comes to drawing illustrations or any other type of graphics. Has both basic and advanced features, which include:

  • excellent interoperability (pen tools, text tools, and shape tools, etc.);
  • fully anti-aliased display;
  • editing, aligning, and spreading of nodes;
  • multi-step gradients.

Since Inkscape is an online resource, you can use it on any platform be it Mac, Windows, or Linux. The nuance worth knowing while working with logo design software is that it may not go smoothly when integrating with Illustrator.

If you want to convert images into vector and bitmap objects, CorelDRAW logo design software is the correct option. Although its features are great and useful, it would not be so easy to manage for a beginner. In such a way, in case you do not have previous experience with other editing tools, the process might get a little complicated.

CorelDRAW pro subscription costs $602, a week free trial is available. But as was aforementioned, it is a wonderful tool for superior vector files and logo sketches.

Probably, if there were a need to name the most popular and overused logo design software, it would be Canva. It has been evolving its tool collection since 2013 and now has achieved great results. This application is famous for its availability to beginners and highly understandable convenient interface. Canva’s features are numerous, moreover, they also offer a fair quantity of templates for logo making.

Anyway, it has some disadvantages too. It may get a PC slowed down. Canva comes in free and premium versions. The last is $9.95 per month.

GIMP

GIMP is a marvelous open-source logo design software. It runs in a raster environment. Its interface is easy to maintain for those who are only making first steps in graphic design, yet specialists can apply it in their project creation as well.

The interesting fact about GIMP is that, among all other logo makers, it was taking part in the original 1998 design of Google's logo. So, do not hesitate to use it in your succeeding job.

The next logo design software program is Adobe Spark. It is another Adobe brand tool. It is pretty versatile and, therefore, multi-purpose. Adobe Spark comes in $59.99 per year for an individual user and $239.88 if you are planning to work in a team.

One more logo design software by Adobe’s Creative Suite and the very last in our list. As any other app in the suite Adobe Photoshop has an excessively complex interface.
